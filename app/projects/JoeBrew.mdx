## Overview

**JoeBrew** is a modern web platform for a coffee shop that offers **coffee, milk tea, frappes, and takoyaki**.  
The application provides an **interactive menu, online ordering system, and product information**, giving customers an easy and enjoyable way to browse and order their favorite items.  

Through its intuitive interface, users can explore menu items, view prices, and place orders online, improving customer engagement and convenience.

---

## Key Features

- **Interactive Menu**: Browse coffee, milk tea, frappes, and takoyaki offerings.  
- **Online Ordering**: Place orders directly through the web application.  
- **Product Details**: View ingredients, prices, and images for each item.  
- **Customer Engagement**: Ratings, reviews, and recommendations.  
- **Responsive Design**: Accessible across desktop and mobile devices.  

---

## Technologies Used

- **HTML5 & CSS3** → User interface and responsive design.  
- **JavaScript / React** → Interactive components and order handling.  
- **Node.js / PHP** → Backend logic and server-side functionality.  
- **MySQL / MongoDB** → Database for products, orders, and customer data.  
- **API Integrations** → Payment gateways and notifications.  

---

## Challenges & Learnings

- Designing an **intuitive menu interface** for multiple types of products.  
- Implementing **online ordering** with real-time updates.  
- Ensuring **cross-device responsiveness** for seamless customer experience.  
- Managing **product data and inventory** efficiently in the backend.  

---

## Outcome

The **JoeBrew platform** provides a **convenient and interactive way for customers to browse and order coffee, milk tea, frappes, and takoyaki**.  
It demonstrates how web technologies can streamline ordering, improve customer engagement, and support a small business with a functional online presence.
